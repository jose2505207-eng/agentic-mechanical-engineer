# Simulation Engine & Failure Prediction Engine

## Simulation Engine

- **Kinematic Analysis:** reachability, workspace, motion constraints
- **Dynamic Analysis:** forces, accelerations, momentum
- **Structural Analysis:** stress estimation, deflection estimation, load paths
- **Collision Analysis:** self collisions, environmental collisions
- **Battery Analysis:** runtime, consumption
- **Payload Analysis:** capacity, torque requirements
- **Stability Analysis:** center of gravity, tip-over risk

## Failure Prediction Engine

Detect:
- Structural overload
- Motor overload
- Joint overload
- Battery limitations
- Stability issues
- Excessive weight
- Weak geometry
- Manufacturing concerns

Output:
- Failure score
- Risk analysis
- Recommended fixes
