# Startup Positioning & Market Expansion

## Positioning

Do NOT position as: "Text to CAD"
Do NOT position as: "Robot Generator"

Position as: **AI Mechanical Engineering Copilot** or **AI Mechanical Engineer**

Customers pay for:
- Reduced engineering time
- Reduced risk
- Faster prototyping
- Lower development cost

## Market Expansion Stages

1. **Stage 1** — 3D Printable Products
2. **Stage 2** — Robotics
3. **Stage 3** — Industrial Automation
4. **Stage 4** — Agriculture Equipment
5. **Stage 5** — Manufacturing Machinery
6. **Stage 6** — Medical Device Engineering Assistance
7. **Stage 7** — General Physical System Design
