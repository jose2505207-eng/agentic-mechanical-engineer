# Material Intelligence System

## Supported Materials

**Plastics:** PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, Nylon, Polycarbonate, Carbon Fiber Nylon

**Metals:** Aluminum 6061, Aluminum 7075, Steel, Stainless Steel, Titanium

**Composites:** Carbon Fiber, Fiberglass

## Recommendations Based On
- Strength
- Weight
- Cost
- Fatigue
- Corrosion
- Thermal performance

## Output
- Recommended material
- Reasoning
- Safety factor
- Cost estimate
