# Core Capabilities

## 1. Natural Language Engineering
Convert human language into engineering specifications.

Examples: payload, reach, speed, runtime, weight, cost, materials, manufacturing constraints

## 2. Requirements Engineering
Extract:
- **Functional Requirements** — e.g., must lift 10 kg, must travel 5 km
- **Physical Constraints** — e.g., must fit inside 500 mm cube, must weigh under 10 kg
- **Manufacturing Constraints** — e.g., 3D printable, CNC manufacturable
- **Business Constraints** — e.g., cost under $500, use existing motors

## 3. Concept Design
Generate:
- Product concepts
- Mechanism concepts
- System architecture

## 4. System Architecture Engine
Supports:

**Robotics:** Mobile Robots, ARM Robots, SCARA, Delta, Cartesians, Surgical Robots, AMRs, AGVs

**Vehicles:** Rovers, Drones, Inspection Platforms

**Industrial Systems:** Inspection Cells, Manufacturing Stations, Automation Equipment

**Agricultural Systems:** Crop Inspection, Harvesting Robots, Sprayers
